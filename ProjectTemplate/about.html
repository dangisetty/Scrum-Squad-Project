About the Scrum Squad Project

The Scrum Squad Project is a collaborative platform designed to improve communication between employees and company leadership. Our goal is to create a transparent, structured way for organizations to listen, respond, and improve.

Employees are empowered to provide meaningful, actionable feedback about workplace challenges, processes, and overall company culture. At the same time, company administrators can track trends, identify priority issues, and share progress updates on how those concerns are being addressed.

By visualizing feedback and progress in one centralized system, Scrum Squad helps organizations move beyond one-time surveys and toward continuous improvement driven by real employee input.
